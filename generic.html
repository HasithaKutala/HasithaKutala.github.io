1.Have Large Language Models solved Natural Language Processing?

I had the pleasure of attending an insightful talk by Professor Yulan He at the King's Festival of Artificial Intelligence. The event explored the advancements in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and the capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, GPT-4 in various NLP tasks. Professor He delved into several exciting topics, including using ChatGPT in adverse drug effect event extraction for pharmacovigilance and universal information extraction. Additionally, she discussed its applications in contrastive opinion extraction, automated scoring of science exams, and knowledge distillation. The talk also touched upon essential aspects like NLP model efficiency, model interpretability, and the development of a new evaluation framework. As a result of this event, I gained valuable insights into the advancements made in NLP and the potential of LLMs like ChatGPT. Integrating machine learning and NLP for text understanding opens exciting possibilities for various domains, including healthcare and education.

2.MLOps Meetup: Unveiling Insights into LLMs and Model Serving Strategies

I attended an MLOps meetup focused on Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), exploring the potential and challenges posed by advanced AI models. Here's a summary: 1. The IQ of AI: Measuring Intelligence in LLMs by Dr. Jodie Burchell Dr. Burchell dissected Large Language Models (LLMs) and discussed claims of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). The talk emphasized systematic intelligence measurements, exploring GPT-4's capabilities and limitations. Key takeaways included the fear of AGI and the need for models to excel in reasoning, planning, problem-solving, abstract thinking, comprehension of complex ideas, learning from experience, and possessing a theory of mind. 2. Building Open-Source LLM Applications by Christopher Samiullah Christopher Samiullah provided a roadmap to harness the potential of open-source Large Language Models. The talk showcased practical examples of enhancing capabilities through Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) and optimizing local models with highly optimized code, quantization, and GPU optimization. 3. ML Model Serving Strategies by Ramon Perez Ramon Perez delivered a comprehensive overview of ML model serving strategies in MLOps, focusing on the evaluation, deployment, and monitoring stages. The talk highlighted three serving methods and deployment strategies, offering concise yet insightful content that sparked my interest in exploring more in the realm of ML deployment!
